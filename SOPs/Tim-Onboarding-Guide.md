## How to Approach Customer Support

**1. Technical Issues**
- "I can't log in"
- "The dashboard isn't loading"
- "I can't see my balance"

**How to solve:**
- Check TeleTrade dashboard for account status
- Try basic troubleshooting first
- If it's a serious bug → escalate to **Mani** (Dev)

**2. Broker/Payment Questions**
- "How do I withdraw?"
- "Which broker do you use?"
- "Are there fees?"

**How to solve:**
- Check OneFX broker details (Nat can help)
- Explain the payment process
- Keep it simple & clear

**3. Strategy/Trading Questions**
- "What strategy do you trade?"
- "How much should I deposit?"
- "Is this risky?"

**How to solve:**
- Give general guidance (keep it friendly)
- If they need advanced advice → escalate to **Noel** or **Livio**
- Your job is support, not strategy advice

## Retention Outreach (The Big Job)

### What is Retention?
Retention = reaching out to customers who haven't been active recently to get them back into trading (and depositing again).

### How It Works

**Step 1: You receive a list from Nat**
- Nat sends you a WhatsApp message with customer names
- Example: "John Doe, Maria Garcia, Ahmed Hassan" (and dates)

**Step 2: Find them in TeleTrade**
- Open TeleTrade Dashboard
- Search for the customer name
- Check: Last login date, Account balance, Trading activity

**Step 3: Craft your message**
Example messages:
- "Hey John! 👋 Long time no see. How's trading going? Any questions I can help with?"
- "Hi Maria, just checking in. Haven't seen activity in a while. Anything blocking you from trading? I'm here to help."
- "Ahmed, missing your trading! What can I do to get you back active?"

**Step 4: Understand their issue**
They might say:
- "I lost money and gave up" → Encourage them to start small again
- "I don't have funds" → Explain they can start with small deposits
- "I forgot my password" → Help them reset
- "I'm happy but just busy" → No action needed, just reconnect

**Step 5: Document everything**
- Open TeleTrade for that customer
- Add a note: "Contacted 2026-04-18, said lost motivation, encouraged small re-entry"
- Mark status: "Outreach Attempted", "Re-deposit planned", "Not interested", etc.

### Retention KPIs (What Lencjs will track)
- Customers contacted: ___
- Successful re-deposits: ___
- Retention rate: ___% (re-deposited / contacted)
