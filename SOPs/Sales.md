# Sales SOPs

## Cold Lead Approach
1. Personalized message with value prop
2. Ask qualifying questions
3. Demo VIP Community free
4. Close to Trial

## Objection Handling
- "Too Expensive" → LAT ROI Calculator
- "Not Enough Time" → VIP Community (passive)
- "Skeptical" → MyFXBook Proof + Testimonials

## Closing Timeline
- Day 1-3: Interest Phase
- Day 3-7: Demo & Education
- Day 7-14: Close or Follow-up Loop
